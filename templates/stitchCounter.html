• Stitch Counter allows users to keep track of their knitting or crochet projects in two ways:
    1. Through a single counter which can be used to track stitches or rows and
    2. Through a double counter which has a counter for stitches and a counter for rows.
  • Stitch Counter has a library system that allows users to save counters and come back to them later.
  • Stitch Counter's theme can be customized through the settings page. Users have access to 6 different color themes.
