Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

Overview

Stitch Counter, developed by Anna Harrison, does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or non-personal user data. The app works entirely offline and all information remains on your device.

If you have questions, contact us at: googleconsole@harrisonsoftware.dev

Data Collection

The app does not collect any data, including:

  • Personal information
  • Device or advertising identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash or diagnostic data

There are no user accounts or sign-in features.

Local Storage

The app allows users to save crochet or knitting projects and counters:

  • All data is stored locally on the device only using Android's Room database
  • No data leaves your device
  • The developer has no access to user-created content
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data

Third Parties & Advertising

  • No third-party services collect data
  • No analytics SDKs are used
  • No advertisements are shown
  • No data is shared or sold

Children

The app is suitable for a general audience. It does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Security

All data you save in the app stays on your device. Android keeps each app's files separate, so other apps cannot access your projects or counters.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date will reflect any changes.
